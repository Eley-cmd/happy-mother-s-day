A Letter for Inay

Dearest Mom,

There are no words grand enough to hold everything you are to me — but I will try, because you deserve every attempt.

You are the warmth I run toward on cold days, the voice that steadies me when nothing else does. You taught me that love is not just a feeling — it is showing up, every single day, even when it is hard.

Thank you for every sacrifice I never saw, every prayer whispered after lights-out, every hug that lasted exactly as long as I needed it to.

I carry you with me — in my laugh, in my stubbornness, in the way I refuse to give up. You are woven into who I am.

Happy Mother's Day. I love you more than any letter can say.

With all my heart,
Your child
